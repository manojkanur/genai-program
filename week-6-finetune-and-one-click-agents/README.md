# Week 6 — Train Your Own AI & Ship It

**Goal:** Make a custom AI that fits your use case, and package it so anyone can use it with one click.

## What you'll learn
- **Fine-tuning** — teach AI your style, your domain, your data
- **LoRA / QLoRA** — cheap fine-tuning that runs on a normal GPU (or Colab)
- **Datasets** — how to prepare training data
- **One-click agents** — package your AI so anyone can run it easily

## Your project this week
- Fine-tune a small AI on your own data (could be your writing, your business FAQs, anything)
- Package it as a simple app anyone can install and use

## Folders
- `01-fine-tuning-basics` — when (and when not) to fine-tune
- `02-lora-qlora` — cheap fine-tuning
- `03-datasets` — preparing your data
- `04-one-click-ai-agents` — package and share

🎉 **You made it!** You can now build, train, and ship AI products.
